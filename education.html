Research for climate solutions

We’re galvanizing the campuses in our global university system to work together to find faster, smarter solutions to the climate change problems people face. By partnering with industry, government, and other universities around the world, we’re able to bring more passion and purpose to problem-solving—and a results-driven mindset that moves discovery through to commercialization.

Co-design IP policies that benefit all partners to ignite new climate industries

Leverage Northeastern's incubator to launch products and joint ventures to fight climate change

In the classroom, and beyond

At Northeastern, learning is personalized and experiential, a perfect mix for tackling the thorny problems caused by climate change. You can create your academic path based on the problems you want to solve, integrating classroom learning with professional experiences—in research, service, policy, business—so deepening your knowledge, gaining new skills, and putting ideas into action toward the sustainability issues you care about.
